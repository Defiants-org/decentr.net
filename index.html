Your data is value. Use it to pay and trade online.

Data is the new currency. Companies have been trading your data for more than a decade. Raw user data which actually belongs to you.

Decentr shakes up this status quo by delivering a web browser experience that gives the data you generate a payable value, accustomed to your surfing style.

DECENTR WEB BROWSER ADD-ON

What are the advantages of user data as payable value and how does it work?

1 On Decentr you own your personal user data; each visit online remains part of your personal ID, which is stored securely on your DecID.

2 Securely stored data has the same “value store” properties as money because secure data is an exchangeable “currency”.

3 Your “Personal Data Value” (PDV) functions as your personal “exchange rate”, which can be used to pay and trade online.

4 Your PDV fluctuates: positive engagement will see a corresponding rise in your PDV, and negative engagement will see a similar fall.

Decentr web 3.0 architecture

Down the "digital" rabbit hole


Decentr's web browser add-on is a “portal to Web 3.0”. By creating a bridge between the internet user and applications developed with distributed ledger technology (DLT); individuals can now connect with user-centric applications, which enables them to manage and trade their "Personal Data Value".

1 The performance of a high quality UI/UX ensures your habits of using internet can adapt effortless.

2 Decentr's web browser Add-on turns all the user-centric backend activity into a frontend web browser experience.

3 A user-centric DLT ecosystem enhances interactivity between you and other user-centric applications.

4 User-Data stored on a decentralised cloud storage endorse the resilience, turning more secure and faster the more it scales.

5 The basis of the system ensures a tamperproof protection whenever you validate the activity of others.
